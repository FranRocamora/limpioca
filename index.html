Who We Are

LIMPIOCA
INTERNACIONAL, C.A.

Since 1970, Limpioca Internacional, C.A. has been a national reference in the distribution of products, equipment and tools for industrial development and maintenance. We also cover gardening equipment distribution and industrial brush manufacturing.

We are backed by the exclusive distribution and representation of the best brands, supported by a network of highly qualified dealers across Venezuela, guaranteeing the best price-value ratio through our modern sales concept.
