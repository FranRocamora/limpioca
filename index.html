LIMPIOCA
INTERNATIONAL

Since 1970, Limpioca International has been a reference in the distribution of products, equipment and tools for industrial development and maintenance. Present in Venezuela, Aruba and the Dominican Republic, we bring world-class industrial solutions to three nations.
